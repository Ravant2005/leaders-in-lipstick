Leaders in Lipstick – Website Web Flow & Architecture Guide
1. Website Navigation Structure
Primary Navigation Menu

1. Home
2. About Leaders in Lipstick
3. Programs
4. Corporate Solutions
5. Community
6. Events & Summits
7. Success Stories
8. Resources
9. Contact

Purpose:
This navigation ensures clarity for three key audiences:
• Women professionals and leaders
• Corporate organisations seeking training
• Community members and event participants
2. Homepage Structure
Purpose:
The homepage acts as the central entry point introducing the Leaders in Lipstick ecosystem and directing users to programs, events, community, and corporate solutions.

Key Sections:
• Hero Banner – Clear positioning statement and CTA (Join the Community / Explore Programs)
• About Snapshot – Introduction to Leaders in Lipstick
• Leadership Programs – Highlights of flagship programs
• Corporate Training Solutions – Programs for organizations
• Upcoming Events & Summits
• Community Membership Benefits
• Success Stories / Testimonials
• Media & Recognition
• Newsletter Signup
• Footer with quick links

Primary CTAs:
• Join Community
• Explore Programs
• Register for Events
• Partner With Us
3. About Leaders in Lipstick
Purpose:
Explain the vision, mission, and credibility of the platform.

Content Sections:
• Founder Story
• Vision & Mission
• Global Impact & Initiatives
• Leadership Team
• Partner Organizations
• Media Coverage

Outcome:
Build trust and establish authority.
4. Programs Architecture
Purpose:
Present leadership development offerings similar to structured service models used by corporate training platforms.

Program Categories:

1. Leadership Development
   - Executive Leadership Programs
   - Women in Leadership Programs
   - Boardroom Leadership Mentorship

2. Personal Branding
   - Personal Brand Building
   - LinkedIn Authority Building
   - Executive Presence Training

3. Professional Growth
   - Communication & Influence
   - Strategic Thinking
   - Negotiation Skills

4. Entrepreneur Leadership
   - Founder Leadership Programs
   - Startup Leadership Development

Each program page must include:
• Program overview
• Learning objectives
• Target audience
• Duration
• Outcomes
• Registration CTA
